# 🪔 Sanatan Shiv Shakti — Master AI Image Prompt

A single, **fill-in-the-blanks prompt** you can paste into *any* AI image generator
(ChatGPT / GPT-Image, Google Gemini / Imagen, Midjourney, FLUX, Ideogram, Leonardo,
Canva AI, Adobe Firefly, etc.) to create on-brand **banners, posters, invitation cards,
social posts, member ID cards, volunteer cards, and deity images** for the organization.

> **How to use:** Copy the **Universal Prompt** below, replace every `[BRACKETED]`
> value with your details, delete the lines you don't need, and paste it into your tool.
> For ready-made versions per asset, see the [`templates/`](./templates) folder.

---

## 1. Brand Style Anchor — *always keep this block*

```
STYLE: Premium, modern, devotional and elegant — a spiritual & charitable youth
organization from Howrah, West Bengal, India. Clean, uncluttered, trustworthy,
warm and reverent. NOT old-fashioned or cluttered.

COLOR PALETTE: deep saffron / bhagwa (#C2410C), bright saffron (#F59324),
deep maroon (#7A1E27), temple gold (#C8A248), warm cream (#FFFAF2). Gold accents.

MOTIFS (use tastefully, not all at once): lotus, lit diya (oil lamp), Om (ॐ),
mandala / rangoli patterns, trishul, temple bells, marigold garlands, soft light rays,
faint temple silhouette.

MOOD: serene, auspicious, uplifting, community-focused. Soft golden "godly" lighting,
gentle glow, subtle bokeh. High detail, professional, print-quality.
```

## 2. The Universal Prompt (copy & fill in)

```
Create a [ASSET TYPE] for "Sanatan Shiv Shakti", a non-political spiritual & charitable
youth organization in Howrah, West Bengal.

SUBJECT / FOCUS: [DEITY OR SCENE — e.g. Lord Hanuman in a reverent pose / a lit diya and
lotus / devotees doing kirtan / hands offering food].

COMPOSITION: [LAYOUT — e.g. central deity with a clear empty band at the top and bottom
for text / left third reserved for text, artwork on the right]. Leave clean negative
space for text. Balanced, symmetrical, premium layout.

STYLE: premium, modern, devotional, elegant and clean. Colors: deep saffron (#C2410C),
bright saffron (#F59324), deep maroon (#7A1E27), temple gold (#C8A248), warm cream
(#FFFAF2). Decorate tastefully with [MOTIFS — e.g. lotus, mandala border, diya, soft
gold light rays]. Soft auspicious golden lighting, gentle glow, high detail, print-quality.

ASPECT RATIO: [RATIO — see cheat-sheet, e.g. 4:5 portrait poster].

TEXT TO INCLUDE (only if your tool renders text well — otherwise leave this out and add
text afterward in Canva):
  • Organization: "Sanatan Shiv Shakti — Seva · Sanskar · Sanskriti"
  • Title: "[EVENT / TITLE]"
  • Date: "[DATE]  ([DAY])"
  • Time: "[TIME]"
  • Venue: "[VENUE]"
  • [PROGRAM DESCRIPTION — short]
  • Contact: "[PHONE / WEBSITE]"
  Text language: [English / Hindi / Bengali]. Keep text minimal, elegant, highly legible.

AVOID: clutter, distorted faces or hands, extra limbs, garbled/misspelled text,
watermarks, low quality, cartoonish or disrespectful depictions of deities, modern logos
of other brands, political symbols.
```

## 4. Aspect-ratio cheat-sheet

| Use | Ratio | Notes |
|-----|-------|-------|
| Website / Facebook banner | **16:9** (or 1584×396 for FB cover) | wide; keep text centered |
| Event poster (print A4 / A3) | **3:4** (portrait) | most common print poster |
| Instagram / Facebook post | **1:1** (square) | feed posts |
| Instagram/WhatsApp story/status | **9:16** (portrait) | full phone screen |
| Invitation card | **5:7** portrait *(or 7:5 landscape)* | formal invite |
| Member / Volunteer ID card | **1.586:1** (CR80, 1011×638 px @300dpi) | standard ID card |
| Deity image (for reuse in composites) | **1:1** or **3:4** | generate with empty/simple background |

## 5. ⚠️ Text inside AI images — read this first

Most AI image tools **cannot reliably spell text**, and almost **none** render
**Hindi (Devanagari) or Bengali** correctly. Two safe workflows:

- **Workflow A (recommended, works with any tool):** Generate the **artwork only**
  (no text — delete the "TEXT TO INCLUDE" block). Then add the date/time/venue text
  yourself in **Canva**, PowerPoint, or Photoshop, where you control the exact wording
  and can type Hindi/Bengali correctly. This always looks clean.

- **Workflow B (English text only):** Use **Ideogram** or **GPT-Image**, which render
  short *English* text fairly well. Keep text very short (title + one date line).
  Never trust AI to spell Hindi/Bengali — type that in afterward.

## 6. Respectful deity depiction — guidelines

When the asset shows a deity (Hanuman Ji, Lord Shiva, Ram–Janki, Krishna, etc.):

- Depict in **traditional, reverent iconography**, calm and serene, front-facing or
  classic devotional pose, with a soft halo / divine glow.
- Include the deity's **correct attributes** where relevant: *Hanuman* — gada (mace),
  orange/sindoor body, devoted posture, sometimes carrying Sanjeevani mountain;
  *Shiva* — trishul, crescent moon, Ganga, blue throat, meditative; *Ram* — bow,
  composed and noble; *Krishna* — flute, peacock feather.
- Add the **request: "respectful, traditional, dignified, temple-art / calendar-art
  inspired, no distortion."**
- **Avoid:** extra or malformed limbs, distorted faces, casual/comic poses, mixing
  attributes of different deities, anything irreverent.
- Prefer **illustration / traditional painting style** over hyper-real photos for
  deities (photorealistic faces of gods can look uncanny). Murti (idol) photography
  is also a safe, respectful choice.

## 7. Quality boosters & negative prompt

**Append to boost quality:**
`ultra detailed, sharp focus, professional, balanced composition, elegant typography
space, festival/auspicious atmosphere, soft golden rim light, 4k, print quality`

**Negative prompt (for tools that support one — Midjourney use `--no ...`):**
`blurry, low quality, distorted faces, deformed hands, extra fingers, extra limbs,
garbled text, misspelled words, watermark, signature, cluttered, messy, harsh shadows,
oversaturated, cartoonish, disrespectful, political symbols, other brand logos`
